## 7. Reviews (the biggest trust + ranking multiplier)

Reviews move local rankings and buyer confidence more than almost anything. Ask
every satisfied client; make it one tap.

**Get the review short-link:** in GBP → "Ask for reviews" / "Get more reviews" →
copy the `g.page/r/...` link. Paste it into the templates below.

**WhatsApp request (send after a completed order):**
```
Hi [Name], thank you for your order with Perception Impex — it was a pleasure
supplying your yarn requirement. If you were happy with the quality and service,
a short Google review would mean a lot and helps other manufacturers find us:
[REVIEW LINK]
Thank you! — [Your name], Perception Impex
```

**Email request:**
```
Subject: Quick favour — your feedback on Perception Impex

Dear [Name],

Thank you for choosing Perception Impex for your yarn supply. We'd be grateful for a
brief Google review about your experience — quality, communication, or delivery.
It takes under a minute and genuinely helps other buyers find a reliable supplier:

[REVIEW LINK]

Warm regards,
[Your name]
Perception Impex · +92 300 8209877 · info@perceptionimpex.com
```

**Reply to every review** (positive: thank + reinforce a keyword naturally; negative:
calm, solution-oriented, take it offline). Replying is itself a ranking signal.

Targets: 5 in month 1, 8–10 by month 3, then a steady trickle. Never buy reviews or
incentivize them — Google filters/penalizes both.
