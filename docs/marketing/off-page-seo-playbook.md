## Why this order

Search engines rank a local/B2B business on three things we can still influence:

1. **Google Business Profile completeness + activity** — the single biggest local
   lever, and it compounds (posts + reviews keep it "fresh").
2. **Citation consistency** — the same Name / Address / Phone / Website across many
   trusted directories tells Google the business is real and established.
3. **Genuine mentions & links** — trade bodies (PYMA, KCCI), B2B portals, and real
   community participation. Quality over quantity; one PYMA link > 50 spam links.

We are **not** doing the fake-Reddit-slug parasite tactic (evaluated and rejected —
wrong search demand for B2B yarn, brand risk, and decaying since Google's `num=100`
removal). Community presence here means *real* helpfulness, not decoy pages.
