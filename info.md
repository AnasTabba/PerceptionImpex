## Company Overview

Perception Impex is a Pakistan-based yarn trading and sourcing company established in 2000 by Muhammad Basheer Tabba.

For over 26 years, the company has built strong relationships with some of Pakistan's leading spinning and textile mills, enabling manufacturers and exporters to secure high-quality yarn at competitive rates with reliable delivery schedules.

Perception Impex specializes in sourcing cotton, PC, CVC, and other yarn varieties used in the production of knitwear, polos, hoodies, sweatshirts, trousers, jackets, pullovers, and other textile products.

The company primarily serves textile manufacturers, garment exporters, knitting units, weaving units, fabric producers, and home textile businesses.
